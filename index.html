About Green Day

Green Day is an american rock/punk band formed in Rodeo, California in 1987 by lead vocalist Billy Joe Armstrong and bassist Mike Dirnt. The drummer Tre Cool later joined in 1990. They had their big break in 1994 with their major-label debut "Dookie" release through Reprise Records, which eventually shipped over 10 million copies in the U.S., starting their successful career.
